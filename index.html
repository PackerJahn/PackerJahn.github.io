What is the Gospel Truth and why is it so great?

The Term "Gospel" can be defined as "Good News" and quite frankly in this crazy world I could use some good news right now. And the fact is that the Bible has one big exciting piece of news for everyone! Despite our messed up lives the truth of the matter is God has created a way back to Him.

The Bible isn't boring

One of the most common rejections of the Bible is that its outdated and boring. However this could not be farther from the truth. The Bible details God's incredible plan from day one as it survived Worldwide Floods, conquering armies, and the Devil constantly trying to foil God's plan.
